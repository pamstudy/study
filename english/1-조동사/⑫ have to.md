## have to ##
<br>

must와 동일하게 강한 의무  
<br>

    have to/ has to  
    do/ does  
    don't/ doesn't  
<br>

I <span style="color:red">have to</span> succeed : 난 성공해야만 해  
I don't <span style="color:red">have to</span> give up : 난 절대 포기 안해  
Do you <span style="color:red">have to</span> give up? : 너는 포기해야만 하니?
